Detailed Delivery Conditions & Guarantee

  • Rider & Safety
  • All deliveries carried out by verified and experienced Bike Riders.
  • Parcels are handled with care and secured during transit.
  • Parcel Specifications
  • Maximum weight limit is 10 Kg per parcel for the selected categories.
  • Maximum size is limited to what can be safely carried on a bike (e.g., standard backpack or small carton).
  • No prohibited items (e.g., explosives, perishable food requiring specialized handling, money, or valuables) will be accepted.
  • Booking & Distance
  • Rates are calculated based on the shortest estimated road distance between pickup and drop-off.
  • The rates cover a single, direct drop-off only. Multi-stop deliveries require separate bookings.
  • Transparency & Pricing
  • The price paid confirms the booking for the selected distance and weight category.
  • Any required waiting time exceeding 10 minutes at pickup/drop-off may incur a minimal additional fee, communicated beforehand.
  • Cancellation & Refund
  • Cancellations made before the rider is assigned are eligible for a full refund.
  • A partial service fee may be deducted if the rider is already en route to the pickup location.
