Service Terms & Conditions

Please read our terms carefully to ensure a smooth service experience.

  • Single Property Focus
  • All listed prices are strictly for servicing the sewer line of **one single property**. Connections or blockages originating from a neighbor's property are not included.
  • Additional Neighbor/Shared Lines
  • If the blockage involves a **shared line** or requires work on a neighbor's connection, an **extra charge will apply**. This must be discussed and approved with our company representative before work begins.
  • Complex Multi-Line Systems
  • Properties with **multiple sewer lines** feeding into the main street line (e.g., large complexes, multiple tenant buildings) are **not covered** by standard pricing. A custom quote will be provided after inspection.
  • Service Scope Confirmation
  • Our technicians will assess the situation upon arrival to **confirm the scope of work** and **finalize the price** before starting any job.
